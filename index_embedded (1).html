Why Sapphire

Cut Out the Middleman

Supermarkets markup quality beef significantly. We source directly, process professionally, and sell straight to you.

Our Family Feedlot

Every steer is raised at Smithfield Cattle Company — our own family feedlot. You know exactly where your beef comes from, because we own the whole operation from paddock to box.

Professionally Processed

Hung 7–10 days post-slaughter for optimal tenderness. Every cut is professionally processed, vacuum sealed, and clearly labelled by cut and weight.

No Supermarket Markup

Retail beef passes through processors, distributors, and supermarkets before reaching you. We eliminate every step in between — straight to your freezer.

Real Cuts, No Filler

No offcuts. No low-value fillers. Every item in the box is a cut your family will actually use — from eye fillet down to everyday mince and sausages.

Freezer-Ready

Vacuum sealed for maximum freshness and freezer life. Receive chilled, store as needed. A fully stocked freezer that saves you from last-minute shopping.

Bulk Buying Advantage

Buying a whole box means you're buying at bulk rates. The kind of pricing only available when you know a farmer — now available to everyone.

Where It Comes From

Smithfield Cattle Company — Our Family Feedlot

Sapphire Beef Box exists because of Smithfield Cattle Company — the family feedlot behind every box we sell. We're not buying from a broker or sourcing from a commodity market. The cattle are ours, raised by us, on our property.

That single fact changes everything. We control the feeding programme, the animal welfare, the processing timeline, and ultimately the quality in your box. No surprises. No shortcuts. Full transparency from the feedlot gate to your front door.

The Process

Farm to Your Freezer

Five straightforward steps from paddock to plate.

1

Raised at Smithfield

Cattle are grain-fed for 100 days at Smithfield Cattle Company, our family-owned feedlot.

2

Process & Hang

Professionally slaughtered and hung 7–10 days for peak tenderness and flavour.

3

Pack Into Boxes

Each cut is vacuum sealed, labelled, and packed into standardised 20kg boxes.

4

Delivered or Collected

Boxes are delivered chilled or available for pickup — your choice.

5

Fill Your Freezer

Stock your freezer with months of quality beef at a fraction of retail cost.
